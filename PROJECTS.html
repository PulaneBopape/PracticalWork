MAIN FOCUS


We support the limb difference community and educate the individuals that wish to impact it.

We use 3D printing to provide free recreational prosthetic devices which are designed with children in mind.

We host events which bring together the community and show children with limb differences that they are not alone.

At the heart of our organization is an incredible team of students, community members and care providers who are driven to support the limb difference community.

This diverse and vibrant group drives us to continually improve the services and support we provide.
